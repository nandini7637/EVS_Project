About the Project





The idea of this project is to explain nuclear energy, its history, how nuclear power is generated and its impact on our environment while also explicate the risks associated with it. Nuclear disasters like Fukushima, Chernobyl, and Windscale are covered in detail and aspects of nuclear disaster management is also discussed. Through this project conclusion may be drawn that nuclear power has its own advantages and disadvantages and safety should be the first priority in order to generate power by this resource. Through this project the most controversial Nuclear Technology and it's role intensifies as the climate crisis worsens . Among the highlights of the energy, there are some challenges too. But the prime concern will always remain to be the safely and security while dealing with Nuclear Energy Resources.
